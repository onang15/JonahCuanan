CCS Payment Monitoring System

The CCS Payment Monitoring System is a WordPress-based platform designed to streamline student payment tracking for the College of Computer Studies. It replaces manual processes with automated transaction logging and real-time balance updates. Administrators can easily input payments, while students receive instant email confirmations. A cron job runs on the 1st of every month to automatically send payment reminders, improving communication and consistency. This reduces errors, delays, and administrative workload. The system ensures transparency, accuracy, and efficiency in managing student financial records.

Chikungunya Virus Awareness Seminar

This comprehensive seminar series focused on the Chikungunya virus, an emerging mosquito-borne disease known for causing severe joint pain, fever, and rashes. It was organized exclusively for Grade 12 students to raise awareness and understanding of the virus. I took charge of managing event logistics, which included budgeting, preparing documentation, and arranging meals and tokens for the guest speaker. Overall, the event was a valuable educational experience and a successful collaborative effort.
